A digital tutoring platform

I performed a User Research with the aim to develop a new platform which could be useful for people who need help in the use of digital technologies. I've been first set up an hypotesis of business model and a benchmark on the available competitor tools, then I moved to the research on the users: scenario and personas have been designed starting from the research results.
